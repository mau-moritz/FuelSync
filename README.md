# 🧠 FuelSync

**FuelSync** is a lightweight, responsive web app that helps athletes optimize their nutrition based on their training.

Instead of focusing on calories alone, FuelSync provides **simple, actionable insights** to improve performance for activities like running, HYROX, and endurance training.

---

## 🚀 Overview

FuelSync works as a **smart nutrition companion**:

- Log what you eat 🍽️  
- Record your training 🏃  
- Get clear suggestions to improve your fueling ⚡  

The goal is to remove complexity and give users **practical guidance they can actually use**.

---

## 🎯 Who It's For

- Runners (beginner → intermediate)  
- HYROX / hybrid athletes  
- Young, active people who want simple guidance  
- Anyone tired of overly complex tracking apps  

---

## ✨ Features

- 🥗 **Quick Food Logging**  
  Simple and fast meal input designed for mobile use  

- 🏃 **Training Input**  
  Log workout type, duration, and intensity  

- 🧠 **Smart Suggestions**  
  Get personalized tips based on your activity  

- 📊 **Weekly Insights**  
  Understand patterns and improve over time  

- 📱 **Responsive Design**  
  Works seamlessly on mobile and desktop  

---

## 💡 What Makes FuelSync Different

Unlike apps like MyFitnessPal or Cronometer, FuelSync focuses on:

- Performance, not just calorie tracking  
- Simplicity over data overload  
- Actionable insights instead of raw numbers  

It complements training platforms like Strava by helping users **fuel their workouts properly**.

---

## 🧩 Philosophy

- Minimal input → meaningful output  
- Built for consistency, not perfection  
- Clear feedback over complex analytics  

---

## 🌱 Vision

FuelSync aims to become a simple daily tool that helps users:

- Train smarter  
- Fuel better  
- Improve consistently  

---
